# Description 
Prison Break is an escape and strategy game inspired by Hunt the Wumpus, with a stealth-focused twist. You play as a prisoner trapped deep inside a high-security facility, where you must try to escape with your life.
The prison is represented as a network of interconnected rooms, forming a grid structure. Each location may contain traps, tools, or NPC’s that influence your survival. Your objective is to navigate through the prison and reach the exit, all while avoiding detection by the prison guard.

# Hazards: 
•	Guards (G):
A guard patrols the prison, hunting for you. If you enter the same location as the guard, you are immediately caught, resulting in game over. The guard may also move or react based on information provided by security systems.
•	Security Cameras (C):
Cameras are installed throughout the facility, and  while they do not harm you directly, entering a room with an active camera will reveal your location to the guard. Nearby cameras may also give subtle warnings, allowing careful players to plan safer routes.

# Tools 
•	Weapons:
The player starts with two weapons, a pepper spray for incapacitating the guard and an emf signal to temporarily disable the cameras nearby, providing brief windows of safety or alternate escape routes. However, weapons are limited and must be used strategically.
•	Escape Tunnels (O):
Hidden tunnels act as unpredictable pathways through the prison. Entering a tunnel will lead you to an alternate location, potentially moving you closer to the exit, or straight into danger. Tunnels are bi-directional and can be used strategically, providing both opportunity and risk.
Gameplay Experience
The game emphasizes strategic thinking, and awareness. Much like in Wumpus Hunt, you wont see threats directly, instead, having to rely on environmental clues to decide your next move. Every turn requires careful consideration, as one wrong decision may alert the guard or trap you with no escape.

# Uniqueness
Our implementation differs from the original through its theme and core objective. Rather than centering the gameplay around hunting and killing the Wumpus, our game uses a prison break theme that shifts the player’s goal, which is no longer to eliminate a single threat, but to carefully navigate the prison environment while avoiding traps and guards. Players must use escape tunnels to reposition themselves strategically and rely on weapons as tools for temporary advantage, emphasizing stealth, planning, and smart decision-making over direct confrontation.
